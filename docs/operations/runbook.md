# Operations Runbook

## Objective

Provide a repeatable operating sequence for Ghost Thermostat BuilderOps activities.

## Standard Lifecycle

1. Intake and requirements confirmation
2. Design and risk review
3. Build and validation
4. Deployment and commissioning
5. Monitoring and maintenance

## Roles and Ownership

- Owner: accountable for completion and quality.
- Reviewer: validates compliance and readiness.
- Operator: executes field steps and logs outcomes.

## Minimum Evidence per Execution

- Task ID or issue reference
- Date/time and environment
- Operator name
- Outcome summary
- Follow-up actions (if any)

## Escalation

Escalate immediately when:

- Safety risk is identified
- Data integrity is uncertain
- A step cannot be completed as documented
